# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in
the VJLive3 community a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability,
ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education,
socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or
sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to providing a community that is free from harassment and
discrimination, and we expect all community members to uphold these values.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful and inclusive**: Use welcoming and inclusive language
- **Being considerate**: Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- **Gracefully accepting constructive criticism**: Focus on what's best for the community
- **Showing empathy**: Offer help to others, especially newcomers
- **Focusing on what's best for the community**: Prioritize collective good over personal interests

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- **Harassment and discrimination**: Any form of harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- **Inappropriate language**: Profanity, slurs, or derogatory comments
- **Personal attacks**: Criticizing individuals rather than ideas
- **Unwelcome advances**: Sexual or romantic attention without consent
- **Disruptive behavior**: Trolling, insulting, or other disruptive conduct
- **Privacy violations**: Sharing others' private information without permission
- **Other conduct**: That could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all community spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories and issues
- Pull requests and code reviews
- Discussions and forums
- Chat channels (Discord, Slack, etc.)
- Mailing lists
- Meetups and events (virtual or in-person)
- Any other official community spaces

## Enforcement

Community members who violate this Code of Conduct may face:

- **Warning**: First offense may result in a private warning
- **Temporary suspension**: Temporary restriction from community spaces
- **Permanent ban**: Permanent removal from the community

The enforcement team reserves the right to take appropriate action based on
the severity and pattern of violations.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it:

1. **Contact the enforcement team**:
   - Email: conduct@vjlive.com
   - Include: Description of incident, date/time, location, people involved

2. **Confidentiality**: All reports will be kept confidential to the extent possible

3. **No retaliation**: Retaliation against reporters is strictly prohibited

4. **Response time**: We aim to respond within 48 hours

5. **Resolution**: We'll investigate and determine appropriate action

The enforcement team is responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct:

- **Lead**: [Name], conduct@vjlive.com

The enforcement team will recuse themselves from cases involving themselves

## Appeals

If you believe enforcement action was taken in error:

1. **Appeal in writing** to conduct@vjlive.com
2. **Include**: Why you believe the action was incorrect
3. **Review**: Appeals will be reviewed by the enforcement team (excluding those involved)
4. **Decision**: Final decision will be communicated within 7 days
